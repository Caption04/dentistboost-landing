You've got questions. We've got answers.

1. What types of lawn installation and maintenance services do you offer?

We offer lawn installation, ground leveling, termite treatment, and ongoing lawn care maintenance services. We have three types of lawn: Kikuyu, Durban, and Buffalo. We also sell lawn bags.

2. What are the prices for your lawn care services, and are there any discounts available?

Our prices vary depending on the service and lawn size. The prices for our lawn types are as follows: - Kikuyu: $2 per square meter (supply and fixing) in and around Harare - Durban: $5 per square meter (supply and fixing) in and around Harare - Buffalo: $6 per square meter (supply and fixing) in and around Harare We also sell lawn bags: - 50kg bag of Kikuyu: $20 - 50kg bag of Durban: $30 - 50kg bag of Buffalo: $35 We offer discounts to clients with 300 squares and above in Harare and 500 squares and above outside Harare, depending on distance and quantity. We supply nationwide, and prices vary based on distance.

3. Where is your company located, and do you serve areas outside of Kwazana?

We're based in Kwazana and serve surrounding areas. We have one of our offices in Norton area and Hertfield, on number 17 Winson Road.

4. Can I schedule a site visit to assess my lawn's needs?

Yes, we offer site visits to assess your lawn's needs and provide a customized quote. There is a fee of $10 for site visits in Harari, which is refundable if we secure a deal with you. We'll deduct the $10 from your total bill if you decide to proceed with our services.

5. How long does the lawn installation or maintenance process typically take?

The duration varies depending on the service and lawn size, but we provide a timeline during the site visit. With a team of 35 professionals, we work efficiently and take our job seriously.

6. Do you offer ongoing lawn care maintenance services?

Yes, we offer customized maintenance plans to keep your lawn healthy. We have contracts with clients like ZB to maintain their lawns for a specified period, such as 18 months.

7. Can I pay in installments or is full payment required upfront?

We offer flexible payment options. Contact us to discuss payment terms.
